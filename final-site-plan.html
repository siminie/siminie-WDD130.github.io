Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help women of all ages find their perfect outfit that makes them feel comfortable in their own body. This site will also provide information on differnt body types and how to find your body type. The site will also have links to other pages which relate to fashion by style and event.

Audience

For women and females of all ages that are looking for ways to make their outfits look stylish and fresh. This fashion guide will provide different styles of fashion and specific body type outfits that help women and females of all ages feel comfortable in their skin.
