Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help women of all ages find their perfect outfit that makes them feel comfortable in their own body. This site will also provide information on differnt body types and a few of the popular fashion styles out there. The site will also have links to other pages such as the home page and a way to contact us with any questions that the audience may have.

Audience

For women and females of all ages that want to discover their sense of fashion and choose a fashion style. This fashion guide will provide different styles of fashion and specific body type outfits that help women and females of all ages feel comfortable in their skin. Some questions that will be answered:

  • How do I find my body type?
  • What style is the best for a spefic age?
  • Do I need to buy specific clothes to fit a fashion style?
